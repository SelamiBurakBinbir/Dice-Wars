How to Play?

  • Objective: Dice Wars is a turn-based strategy and chance game. The goal for each player is to capture every cell on the map.
  • How to Attack: To initiate an attack, the player must first select one of their own cells, then click on an adjacent enemy cell. The numbers in each cell represent the number of dice present.
  • Battle Mechanics: During an attack, both the attacking and defending cells roll a number of dice equal to the number shown in the cell.
  • Attack Outcomes:
    • Failed Attack: If the sum of the defender’s dice is greater than that of the attacker, the attack fails and the attacking cell is reduced to just 1 die.
    • Successful Attack: If the attacking cell’s dice show a higher value than those in the defending cell, the enemy cell is captured. One die remains in the original cell, while the remaining dice move into the captured cell.
  • Restrictions: An attack cannot be initiated from a cell that contains only 1 die.
  • End-of-Turn Bonus: After completing all attacks, the player ends their turn by clicking the "Next Player" button and receives bonus dice equal to the number of cells in their largest connected cell cluster. For example, if the largest cluster contains 10 cells, 10 bonus dice are randomly distributed among the player's cells. The panel displays the number of bonus dice to be received.
  • Dice Limit per Cell: Each cell can contain a maximum of 10 dice.
  • Strategy Tips:
    • Maintain connectivity between cells to maximize bonus dice at the end of the turn.
    • Be cautious with attacks; if the total dice values are equal, the defending cell wins, putting the attacking cell at a disadvantage.
